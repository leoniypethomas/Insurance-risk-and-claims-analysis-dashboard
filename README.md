This project focuses on analyzing insurance risk, claim behavior, and policy patterns using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The analysis helps identify trends across customer demographics and vehicle attributes to support data-driven decision-making in the insurance domain.

Key Highlights
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze insurance risk, claim distribution, and policy trends across customer demographics and vehicle characteristics.
Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query, including feature engineering for derived fields such as Age and Age Group to enable segmentation analysis.
Created DAX measures to calculate key business metrics and enable dynamic filtering through slicers.
Designed KPIs including Total Claim Amount, Total Policies, Average Claim Amount, Claim Frequency, and Gender-wise Segmentation to evaluate risk patterns and claim behavior.
Delivered clear visual insights to support underwriting, risk assessment, and policy optimization decisions.
